

# RackNerd Payment Workaround - 2026-03-28

RackNerd (clientarea.racknerd.com) does NOT allow adding funds to an account balance using PayPal as a one-time transaction. The available 'Add Funds' payment methods are limited to Crypto, Stripe Alipay, Wire Transfer, and UnionPay. Furthermore, the system requires at least one active order to use the 'Add Funds' feature. 

However, PayPal IS available as a payment method for direct invoice payments, as confirmed by viewing the unpaid invoice in the account. For users with a Vanilla Visa card, paying the invoice directly via PayPal (if the card is linked to a PayPal account) remains a viable option, but the specific 'Add Funds' balance workaround is not possible with PayPal.
